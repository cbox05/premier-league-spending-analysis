1. Match-Level Performance Data

Source: football-data.co.uk
Competition: English Premier League
Seasons: 2018–19 through 2022–23
Format: CSV files downloaded manually

The match-level dataset includes:

HomeTeam

AwayTeam

FTHG (Full-Time Home Goals)

FTAG (Full-Time Away Goals)

FTR (Full-Time Result)

These match-level records were aggregated to construct season-level performance metrics including total points, goal difference, and final league position.

2. Transfer Spending Data

Source: Transfermarkt
Section: Premier League “Income & Expenditures”
Seasons: 2018–19 through 2022–23
Format: Tables manually copied and exported to CSV

The following columns were retained:

Club

Expenditure

Income

Balance

Currency values were cleaned and converted into numeric millions during preprocessing.
